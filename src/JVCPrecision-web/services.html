What We Do Here at JVC Precision

With a bed size of 5’ x 10’ our MAZAK CNC laser is capable of cutting up to ¾” thick steel, 3/8” stainless steel, and ¼” aluminum. Here at JVC Precision we offer Mig & Tig welding of aluminum, steel, stainless, copper and brass we also have spot welding capabilities for steel and stainless steel items. We have an extensive inventory of precision tooling for our 145 tonne, 8 axis Amada brake press for your forming needs.
Our CNC Tube bending and Machine shop rounds off our equipment to provide our customers with a one stop facility for their manufacturing needs. At JVC Precision we can utilize AutoCAD, Solid Works, IGS, Unigraphics, Inventor, Mechanical Desktop and many more software formats. Our in house 3D design & drafting services can help take your ideas from concept to reality.
